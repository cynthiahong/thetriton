ERC Student Council Allocated $7,500 For Annual Retreats; Almost Double That of All Other Councils

Natasha Vyhovsky | Feb 28, 2017

The Student Council for Eleanor Roosevelt College (SCERC) allotted $7,500 of its yearly budget to its annual council retreat fund this school year, compared to Warren, Muir, Revelle and Marshall which allocated $3,969, $500, $308 and $473 respectively.

UCSD Groups Unite To Protest Trump's Immigration Ban

Natasha Vyhovsky | Jan 30, 2017

Approximately five hundred people gathered to rally in response to President Trump’s executive order on immigration, banning all refugees and immigration from countries. The march was an effort to build solidarity among students in light of what many are recognizing as a “Muslim ban.”

Natasha Vyhovsky | Jan 20, 2017

Hundreds of UCSD students, faculty members, and staff demanded the University’s denunciation of the Trump presidency during a walk-out strike and public rally. Organized by the LZC, participants attempted to block parking lots and traffic to disrupt the normal flow of the university.
